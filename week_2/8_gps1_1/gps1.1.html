Definition

When two or more coders come together to work on a single script.

How To?

One person is the navigator and one person is the driver

Driver

Driver does the input which should be focused on the code instructed by the navigator. Essentially the driver presses the gas but the navigator says when to turn.

Navigator

The navigator usually takes the passenger seat and is the strategist and guides the driver where to go. Always keeping the bigger picture in mind, but when dictating never getting too ahead of where the driver is.

Benefits

  • Minimize mistakes.
  • More productive.
  • Two people with different perspectives can create efficiencies.
